
## Concepts

1. *Modulo rules*

- `(a+b)%m = (a%m+b%m)%m.`
- `(a*b)%m = (a%m*b%m)%m.`

2. *10e9 + 7*
- it is caluclated as (10^10) + 7

3. *Use of &*
- It is used to take the reference of the argument passed while calling the function. It helps to `call by reference`.
